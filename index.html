You barely manage to slide under the entrance before it slams close. It is pitch dark, but your torch still burns, if weakly. In the distance sits still an immense, dark mass. Before you can make out what or who it is in the dim glow, two bright eyes pop open, and the chamber quakes as you hear thunderous speech. "You who wish to enter, if you are not to be vanquished here, must answer my questions three. At every of your utterances, the structure will shift and rattle under you. Make haste, lest it give way. And do not get ahead of yourself. Well, what do they call me?"

"Good enough. And my swift friend, the Daisy-Adorned? What does he go by?"

"That is.. surprising. And the third of us, him of the Amber coat?"

"It can't be! Is that..?"
