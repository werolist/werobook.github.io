She barely managed to slide under the entrance before it slammed shut. It was pitch dark, but her torch burned still, if weakly. In the distance sat unmoving an immense, dark mass. Before she could make out what it was in the dim glow, two bright eyes burst open, and the chamber quaked, rattled by thunderous speech: "Hah, you there! How did you figure there was more behind the waterfall? Well. You who wish to enter, if you are not to be vanquished here, must answer my questions. At every of your utterances, the structure will shift and agitate under you. Make haste, lest it give way, and do not get ahead of yourself. To begin with, what do they call me?"

"Good enough. And my swift companion, the Daisy-Adorned? What does he go by?"

"That is.. surprising. And the third of us, him of the Amber coat?"

"It can't be! Is that..?"
